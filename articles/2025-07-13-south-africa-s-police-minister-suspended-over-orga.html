jan Senzo e ala mi sona jan Cyril pi laken. Let's break down why this translation works: * **jan Senzo e ala mi sona:** This means "Jan Senzo is on leave now". "Ala" means "on", "mi" means "now", and "sona" means "leave". * **jan Cyril pi laken:** This means "Jan Cyril gave the leave". "Pi" means "by", "laken" means "give leave/permission".
